Spicy Shrimp Tacos

Description

Spicy shrimp tacos are a vibrant and flavorful dish featuring seasoned shrimp served on soft tortillas with fresh toppings and a zesty lime crema.

Ingredients

For the Shrimp Marinade:

  • 1 lb shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons lime juice
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

For the Lime Crema:

  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • 1 teaspoon lime zest
  • Salt, to taste

For the Assembly:

  • 8 small flour or corn tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded red cabbage
  • 1 ripe avocado, sliced
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1 jalapeño, sliced (optional)
  • Lime wedges, for serving

Steps

    Prepare the Marinade:
    • In a bowl, combine the olive oil, lime juice, minced garlic, chili powder, smoked paprika, ground cumin, cayenne pepper, salt, and pepper.
    • Add the shrimp to the bowl, ensuring each piece is well coated with the marinade.
    • Cover and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to infuse the shrimp.

    Cook the Shrimp:
    • Preheat a skillet or grill to medium-high heat.
    • Cook the marinated shrimp for about 2-3 minutes on each side, until they are pink and opaque. Remove from heat.

    Prepare the Lime Crema:
    • In a small bowl, combine the sour cream, mayonnaise, lime juice, lime zest, and salt.
    • Stir until all ingredients are well blended and the crema is smooth.

    Prepare the Toppings:
    • Shred the red cabbage, slice the avocado, and chop the fresh cilantro.
    • Slice the jalapeño, if using, and set aside.

    Warm the Tortillas:
    • Warm the tortillas on a skillet or in the oven until they are soft and pliable.

    Assemble the Tacos:
    • Spread a small amount of lime crema on each tortilla.
    • Add a handful of the cooked shrimp.
    • Top with shredded red cabbage, sliced avocado, fresh cilantro, and sliced jalapeño (if using).
    • Drizzle more lime crema on top if desired.

    Serve:
    • Serve the spicy shrimp tacos immediately with lime wedges on the side for extra zing. Enjoy the bold and vibrant flavors!
